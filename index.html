About

A decade of cutting real stories.

I'm a video editor who's spent the last ten years obsessing over one thing — making footage feel like the story it actually is. Not flashy. Not over-produced. Just clean, natural edits that keep viewers watching.

I've cut my own channels, partnered with creators across YouTube, and worked on short films that needed to land in under three minutes. My niche is YouTube creators making vlogs, skits and sketch content where pacing, timing and tone matter more than transitions.

I run every project on Adobe Premiere Pro for the picture and Logic Pro for audio — so the visuals feel sharp and the sound feels intentional. Always.
